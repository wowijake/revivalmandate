About Revival Mandate

Revival Mandate International is a revival network encompassing the revival mandate institutes, revival mandate publications, and revival mandate ministries.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower communities through innovative solutions and compassionate support. We strive to create meaningful impact and foster positive change.

Our Vision

Our vision is to equip the generation of women through the building of social competence skills. We envision a world where every individual has the opportunity to thrive and contribute to a better society.
