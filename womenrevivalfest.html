Empowering Women Worldwide for Spiritual and Career Growth

About Women RevivalFest

The Women RevivalFest is a dynamic movement that seeks to build a worldwide network of women. It aims at inspiring and empowering women with leadership and management capabilities to attune to contemporary demands of spiritual, business, family and career development.

Women RevivalFest Mandate Statement

The movement endeavors to facilitate effective networking, skill building, and leadership development through a collaborative involvement with inspirational and visionary teachers, mentors, and life coaches from different career professions, theological backgrounds, and business enterprises.

Vision Framework

Spiritual Development

Vision Statement: Build a strong spiritual foundation for women, and catalyze an awakening that orients the generation of women to the fulfillment of their God-given calling.

Main objectives:

  • Develop the capacity of the women ministry to effectively manage and support extreme women involvement in all spiritual aspects.
  • Revitalize the women body and develop functional platforms within the ministry to exercise their gifting.
  • Improve inter-ministerial and multi-sectorial collaboration in the delivery of ministry services and implementation of ministry policies and programs to advance the visibility of women in ministry.

Career and Entrepreneurship Development

Vision Statement: Build a strong economic foundation for the generation of women, a foundation that is dynamic, sustainable, and innovative that will essentially promote women involvement and strengthen their productive capacities.

Main objectives:

  • Instigate lessons on how to survive transitions in life such as education, work, and marriage.
  • Promote the programming of innovative economic development plans that will catalyze changes through campus, regional, national, and transnational programs and projects.

Health and Family Enrichment

Vision Statement: Equip the generation of women through the building of social competence skills, emotional intelligence, health ethics, home management, and general etiquette.

Main objectives:

  • Create a strong focus on and commitment to the well-being of women, including their ethical conduct and health.
  • Institutionalize programs that advance and advocate for effective and efficient parental mentorship styles, family health standards, and better livelihood.

Activities

Women RevivalFest Conferences

RevivalFest Conferences are a biannual international women conference that serves as a holistic forum for empowerment, inspiration, networking with esteemed speakers, and involvement in transformative discussions.

Women RevivalFest Masterclass

The Women RevivalFest Masterclass is a faith-based enrichment program that offers monthly leadership and management training with an emphasis on life skills and higher-order thinking.
